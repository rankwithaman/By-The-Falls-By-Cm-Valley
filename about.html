The Beginning

Born from a Vision of Pure Excellence

"We didn't just build a restaurant. We built a sanctuary where people could rediscover the joy of truly exceptional dining."

By The Falls By CM Valley was born in 2016 from a simple yet profound dream — to create a dining experience in Ludhiana that could rival the finest restaurants in the world. Nestled within the verdant beauty of CM Valley, our founder envisioned a space where the majesty of nature and the mastery of cuisine could coexist in perfect harmony.

What began as an intimate 40-seat establishment has grown into Punjab's most sought-after fine-dining destination, hosting thousands of cherished moments — from first dates to golden anniversaries, from business milestones to family celebrations.

Our Principles

The Philosophy That Guides Everything We Do

Purity of Ingredients

We believe great cuisine begins at the source. Every ingredient we use is sourced with care, sustainability, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Passion in Every Dish

Cooking is an act of love. Our chefs pour their heart into every plate, treating each dish as both a craft and a gift to our guests.

Hospitality as Heritage

In Punjab, guest is God. We honour this sacred tradition by treating every person who walks through our doors as an honoured guest.

Nature as Inspiration

The waterfalls, the valley, the open skies — nature is our greatest muse. Our menu changes seasonally to celebrate what the earth offers us.

By The Falls opens its doors to an overwhelming response, seating 40 guests in an intimate setting beside nature's cascade.

First Expansion

Dining capacity triples to 140 seats. Private dining rooms and outdoor terrace are added, along with a premium bar.
