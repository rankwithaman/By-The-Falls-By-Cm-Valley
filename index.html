Welcome to By The Falls

A Symphony of Flavour & Ambience

"Dining is not merely eating — it is an art, a ritual, a celebration of the finest pleasures life offers."

Set against the breathtaking backdrop of natural waterfall surroundings, By The Falls By CM Valley is Ludhiana's most celebrated luxury dining destination. We blend the richness of Indian heritage with global culinary mastery, all within an atmosphere of timeless elegance.

Every detail — from our hand-picked ingredients to our candlelit table settings — is curated to transport you into a world where time slows, and every bite tells a story.
